Taste The Feeling

Exploring Food Together is a toolkit of simple activities that adults who work with
young children can use, in the classroom or in the home, to help kids learn about
new foods and start building the skills to make healthy food choices. All of the toolkit
activities were designed to integrate with overall curriculum objectives in early childhood education settings.
